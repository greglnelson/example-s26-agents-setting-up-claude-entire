# Godzilla MonsterVerse Movie Trivia

## Godzilla (2014)
- This was the first American Godzilla film since the 1998 Roland Emmerich version and rebooted the franchise from scratch.
- Bryan Cranston filmed his scenes fresh off the success of Breaking Bad.
- The movie intentionally kept Godzilla off-screen for much of the runtime, inspired by Spielberg's approach to Jaws.
- Godzilla's roar was redesigned using a combination of dragging a leather glove across a stringed instrument and other unconventional sounds.

## Kong: Skull Island (2017)
- Set in 1973 to tie into the Vietnam War era and avoid modern military technology that could trivialize the monsters.
- The film confirmed Kong and Godzilla exist in the same cinematic universe (the MonsterVerse).
- John C. Reilly's character was a late addition to the script but became a fan favorite.
- The post-credits scene revealed cave paintings of Mothra, Rodan, King Ghidorah, and Godzilla.

## Godzilla: King of the Monsters (2019)
- Featured classic Toho monsters Mothra, Rodan, and King Ghidorah in an American film for the first time.
- Bear McCreary's score incorporated Akira Ifukube's original 1954 Godzilla theme.
- The Oxygen Destroyer, a key weapon from the original 1954 Japanese film, makes an appearance.
- Over 17 Titans are referenced in the film, though not all appear on screen.

## Godzilla vs. Kong (2021)
- The film was released simultaneously in theaters and on HBO Max due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
- It became the most commercially successful MonsterVerse film at the worldwide box office during its pandemic release.
- The Hollow Earth concept was inspired by classic sci-fi like Jules Verne's Journey to the Center of the Earth.
- Kong's axe is made from a dorsal plate of one of Godzilla's ancestors.
- Deaf actress Kaylee Hottle played Jia, who communicates with Kong through sign language.

## Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire (2024)
- The film leaned more heavily into the Hollow Earth mythology, expanding the underground world significantly.
- It introduced Skar King, a new original Titan antagonist ruling over a hidden ape civilization.
- Kong receives a mechanical prosthetic arm enhancement built from Hollow Earth technology.
- Shimo, an ice-powered Titan, is revealed to be one of the oldest creatures in the MonsterVerse.
- The film broke the record for the biggest opening weekend in the MonsterVerse franchise.
